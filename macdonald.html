MACDONALD

This classic long-running fast food chain is known for its burgers, fries and shakes. McDonald’s has opened restaurants all over the world: the V&A Waterfront branch is situated in the Victoria Wharf Shopping Centre. You can choose to sit and enjoy your meal, or take it away. At McDonald’s the staff have a commitment to quality, service, cleanliness and value for money. At the heart of everything prepared in this restaurant, every ingredient the suppliers provide, and every person that works here, is the focus on delivering the high-quality meals that McDonald’s customers expect.
